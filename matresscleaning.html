House Cleaning


Is your mattress in need of a thorough clean to remove stains, allergens, and odors? Look no further than Domit Cleaning Services for professional mattress cleaning solutions that will leave your mattress clean, fresh, and rejuvenated.

Our Cleaning Process


Vacuuming & Spot Removal


We start by vacuuming the surface of the mattress to remove loose dirt, dust, and debris, preparing it for the deep cleaning process.Stubborn stains and spots are treated individually with specialized cleaning solutions to ensure they are fully removed, leaving your mattress looking spotless.


Steam Cleaning & Extraction


Using advanced steam cleaning equipment, we deep clean your mattress, applying hot water and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to effectively lift away dirt, allergens, and bacteria from deep within the mattress fibers.Our powerful extraction process removes excess moisture along with the cleaning solution, ensuring that your mattress dries quickly and thoroughly.


Deodorization & Sanitization


For added peace of mind, we offer optional sanitization treatments to kill germs, bacteria, and allergens on the surface of your mattress, creating a healthier sleeping environment for you and your family.
